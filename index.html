About Us

Centenary SACCO is a savings and credit co-operative Society Registered in the year 2003 under the Co-operative Societies Act vide registration certificate No. 10068. It was later in the year 2011 licensed as a Deposit Taking Sacco (DTS) by the Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA) The sacco has branches at Intercity Centre Meru (Head office), Chuka, Kinoro, Marimanti, Mutuati and Timau Centenary Sacco aims at bringing affordable credit facilities, credit management skills and drive its members to economic prosperity from whatever economic status.

Why choose Centenary SACCO?

✓ Diverse Branch Network: Convenient locations at Intercity Centre Meru, Chuka, Kinoro, Marimanti, Mutuati, and Timau.

Our Services

  • Kumekucha Loan: Short term loan for organized groups to finance income-generating projects.
    • Up to Kshs 5,000,000 for groups and Kshs 10,000,000 for institutions.
    • Repayment period of up to 24 months with a grace period of 2 months.
  • Microcredit Loan: Short term loan for members of microcredit groups.
    • Payable within 12 months in equal installments.
    • Loan amount up to 10 times one’s deposit, maximum of Kshs 80,000.

  • M-Pesa Paybill Services: Deposit cash, pay loans, and make contributions via M-Pesa.
    • Business No: 700300, enter your Membership No.
  • Mobile Banking Services: Withdraw cash from FOSA accounts by dialing "477#".

  • Classic Loan: Loan for permanent employees channeling salaries through the Sacco.
  • Salary Loan: Short term loan for employees whose salary is processed through the FOSA account.

  • Asset Financing: Loan for acquiring assets like land and vehicles.
  • Instant Loan: Loan for active Sacco members for up to 3.5 times total deposits.
